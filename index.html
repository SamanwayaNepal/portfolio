ABOUT SAMANWAYA NEPAL

Coordinating for a stronger democracy.

A premier, youth-centered, not-for-profit institution headquartered in Kathmandu — founded on the conviction that sustainable social transformation happens when young people are structurally empowered to co-design the civic systems they inherit.

Our Vision

An inclusive, accountable, and resilient democratic society where all young people — irrespective of gender, ethnicity, geography, or background — have the agency, platform, and equity to shape the decisions that govern their lives.

Our Mission

To fortify youth participation across democratic governance and safeguard social inclusion — through leadership incubation, civic literacy, grassroots organizing, and evidence-based cross-sectoral collaboration.

Our global alignment

We engineer projects to drive measurable progress across five priority Sustainable Development Goals.
